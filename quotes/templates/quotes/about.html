About Friedrich Nietzsche

Friedrich Nietzsche (1844–1900) was a German philosopher, cultural critic, and poet. He is best known for his works: Thus Spoke Zarathustra, Beyond Good and Evil, and The Birth of Tragedy. Nietzsche challenged traditional morality, religion, and the foundations of Western philosophy, introducing influential ideas like the Übermensch, the will to power, and the declaration that "God is dead."

About Me

The app’s goal is to provide an engaging way to explore Nietzsche’s philosophical insights through some of his famous quotes.
